Scaffold designers are workers who are qualified to design scaffolds.

  • Designers for certain scaffolds also must be registered professional engineers.
    • Non-mobile scaffolds must not be moved horizontally with workers on them, unless a registered professional engineer designed the scaffold specifically for the move.]. Note: To move mobile scaffolds,
    • Pole scaffolds over 60 feet in height must be designed by a registered professional engineer, and shall constructed and loaded according to that design.
    • Tube and Coupler scaffolds over 125 feet in height must be designed by a registered professional engineer, and must be constructed and loaded according to that design.
    • Fabricated frame scaffolds (tubular welded frame scaffolds, welded end frame scaffolds, walk through frame scaffolds) over 125 feet in height must be designed by a registered professional engineer, and constructed and loaded according to that design
    • Outrigger scaffolds: Scaffolds and scaffold components must be designed by a registered professional engineer, and constructed and loaded per that design .
