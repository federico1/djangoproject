An interior hung suspension scaffold consists of a platform suspended from the ceiling or roof structure by fixed-length supports.

Specific Requirements

  • Interior hung scaffolds must be suspended from roof structures (e.g., ceiling beams).
  • Roof structures must be inspected for strength before scaffolds are erected.
  • Suspension ropes/cables must be connected to overhead supports by shackles, clips, thimbles, or equivalent means.

Non-mandatory Guidelines

  • Bearers must :
    • Have dimensions of 2 x 10 inches.
    • Be used on edge.
  • For an intended maximum load of 25 to 50 lbs. per square foot, the maximum span is 10 feet.
  • For an intended maximum load of 75 lbs. per square foot, the maximum span is 7 feet.
